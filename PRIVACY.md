# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2025

We value your privacy. This policy outlines what data **Lavalink Status Monitor** collects and how it is used.

## 1. Data Collection
We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to function:
- **Server IDs & Channel IDs:** To send status updates to the correct location.
- **Node Configuration:** IP addresses, ports, and passwords provided by you to monitor your Lavalink nodes.
- **User IDs:** To identify admins for command permissions (if applicable).

## 2. Data Usage
- Your data is used solely for the operation of the Bot.
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
- Node credentials (passwords) are stored locally on the host machine and are never transmitted externally except to connect to the Lavalink node itself.

## 3. Data Retention
- Configuration data is stored until you delete the node or the Bot is removed.
- Logs may be kept temporarily for debugging purposes.

## 4. Your Rights
- You can request the deletion of your data by removing the Bot from your server or using the appropriate delete commands (e.g., `/lavalink-remove`).

## 5. Contact
For privacy concerns, please reach out via the `/support` command.
